FIFA Skill Moves

Learn how to perform FIFA skills easily!

Rainbow Flick

Jump over defenders with style!

  1. Stand still or move slowly.
  2. Drag the ball back with your ← (Left Stick).
  3. Flick the ball forward with your → (Right Stick).
  4. Flick ⏺️ (R2) to make your player jump and go past the defender.

Elastico

Quickly shift ball direction!

  1. Push the ball right with → (Right Stick).
  2. Immediately flick it back opposite direction with ← (Right Stick).
  3. Use ⏺️ (R2) to accelerate quickly after the move.

Heel to Heel Flick

Change direction at speed!

  1. Flick the Right Stick ↑ (Forward).
  2. Quickly flick it again forward with ↑ (Right Stick).
  3. Press ⏺️ (R2) to burst forward with speed.

Ball Roll

Glide past defenders easily!

  1. Hold ← or → (Left Stick).
  2. Keep the ball moving slowly alongside your foot.
  3. Use ⏺️ (R2) to accelerate if needed.

Roulette

Spin around defenders stylishly!

  1. Rotate the Right Stick ⏺️ (Clockwise or Counter-clockwise).
  2. Start at the bottom and roll around either side.
  3. Use ⏺️ (R2) to accelerate through the spin.
